HOW TO GET INSTAGRAM BUSINESS ACCOUNT ID
================================================================================

1. Go to: https://developers.facebook.com/tools/explorer/
2. Select your Instagram app
3. Click "Generate Access Token"
4. Run query: me/accounts?fields=instagram_business_account
5. Copy the instagram_business_account.id value
6. Add to .env.local:
   INSTAGRAM_BUSINESS_ACCOUNT_ID=your_id_here

Detailed instructions: REQUIREMENTS.md
